People also ask
Is the Amazon river the largest river in the world?

The Amazon River is the largest river in the world by volume of water discharged and the second longest river in the world. It has the largest drainage basin in the world, about 7,050,000 square kilometres (2,720,000 sq mi).

Source: National Geographic
Which is largest Amazon or Nile?

The Amazon River is larger than the Nile in terms of water volume. The Amazon discharges about 209,000 cubic meters of water per second, while the Nile discharges about 2,800 cubic meters per second. However, the Nile is generally considered longer at 6,650 kilometers compared to the Amazon's 6,400 kilometers.

Source: World Atlas
Who is the largest river in the world?

The Amazon River is the largest river in the world by volume, discharging about 209,000 cubic meters of water per second. It contains more water than the next seven largest rivers combined and represents 20% of the world's total river flow.

Source: Encyclopedia Britannica
Which article is used before Amazon River?

The definite article "the" is used before Amazon River. For example, "The Amazon River flows through South America." This is the standard convention for river names in English.

Source: English Language Usage
